A clean, modern habit tracking application with a Notion-style UI. Track your daily habits, build streaks, and visualize your progress with an intuitive calendar view and detailed statistics.

## üöÄ Getting Started

The app is already running! Simply open your browser to http://localhost:3000

### Creating Your First Habit
1. Click the **"Add Habit"** button in the top right
2. Enter a habit name (e.g., "Morning Meditation")
3. Optionally add a description (e.g., "10 minutes of mindfulness")
4. Choose a color to organize your habits
5. Click **"Create Habit"**

### Tracking Daily Progress
1. From the **Today** view, see all your habits for the current day
2. Click the checkbox next to a habit to mark it complete
3. Watch your completion count and streak badges update automatically
4. Click again to uncheck if you made a mistake

### Viewing Your Calendar
1. Click the **Calendar** tab
2. Select a habit from the buttons at the top
3. See all your completions for that habit across the month
4. Click any date to mark/unmark that day
5. Use arrows to navigate between months

### Analyzing Your Stats
1. Click the **Stats** tab
2. View your overview cards showing key metrics
3. Scroll down to see individual habit performance
4. Track your streaks and completion rates
